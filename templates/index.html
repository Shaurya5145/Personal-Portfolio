About Me

My Story

I wrote my first Python script at 13 – it was supposed to calculate my cricket batting average, but ended up crashing my computer! From that slightly embarrassing start, I've come a long way to building full-stack AI tools like location-based café finders and content management platforms.

Being in 11th grade means juggling school assignments with late-night coding sessions. Sometimes I get so absorbed in debugging that I forget about my physics homework (sorry, mom!). I'm drawn to projects that combine machine learning with practical web applications – there's something satisfying about making complex AI feel simple for users.

Outside the Code

Cricket is my first love – I play for my school team and dream of the perfect cover drive. In the kitchen, I'm still learning (my attempt at homemade pasta was... interesting). And yes, I do karaoke, though my friends might disagree with my song choices. These experiences teach me patience, creativity, and that failure is just another step toward getting it right.
